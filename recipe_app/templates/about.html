Recipe Genius was created in 2019 by Kyle Stanley, a home cook, who was tired of forgetting ingredients during his weekly trips to the grocery store. Instead of an unordered, not aggregated list of items to purchase scrawled on a piece of paper, Recipe Genius categorizes, groups, and orders ingredients in a logical way making the trip to the supermarker a breeze.
Kyle works in Data Science and loves to make pasta and other elaborate dishes.
